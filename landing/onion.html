How Relays Work
Stealth Addresses
Each payment uses a one-time stealth address derived via ECDH -- the real recipient never appears on-chain. Your stealth code contains scan + spend pubkeys that senders use to derive unique addresses.
Relay Discovery
Relay nodes announce themselves on Nostr. You can browse available relays, see their fees and capacity, and enable your own node as a relay to earn fees for forwarding payments.
Nostr Coordination
Relay discovery and encrypted blobs travel through Nostr relays as encrypted events. No server, no account -- just encrypted P2P signaling over a decentralized relay mesh.
